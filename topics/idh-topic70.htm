Those who are in the path of His Majesty, sincerely performing sadhana twice a day regularly, the thought of Paramapurus’a will be realize in his lifetime.  His liberation is now a sure guarantee.  Therefore every Majii will have to perform sadhana twice a day invariably.  That His loving Supreme Grace must be receive constantly, and everyone is enable to enjoy the eternal blessedness under the loving shelter of the Lord.

 

Verily is this the Supreme Command of the Lord.  Without the Ten (10) Divine Principles, Spiritual development is impossible.  Hence the Lord’s command is to follow the Divine Principles of Sadhana.  Disobedience to this command is nothing but to throw oneself into the tortures of animal life for crores of years.  And wait for the loving spiritual touch of the Lord, that he may resume back again to its human form.

 

Therefore, it is the Supreme Truth that without wholeheartedly accepting the Lord as his Personal Liberator and Controller of the universe, all the efforts of attaining the Supreme goal could not be fulfilled.  Then his mental structures will become crudified and blot out to remain one with the dust of the earth.

 

So, it is the bounden sacred duty of every Majiis to endeavor to bring all to the path of bliss.  Verily is this a part and parcel of service to God— to lead others along the path of His Majesty.

 

 

Hari Om Maheshvara
